About Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a flavorful, one-pot dish popular in West Africa. It is typically made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. This dish is a staple in countries like Nigeria, Ghana, and Senegal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, curry powder, thyme, and bay leaf. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add rice and stir to coat with the tomato mixture.
  4. Add chicken broth, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until rice is cooked.
