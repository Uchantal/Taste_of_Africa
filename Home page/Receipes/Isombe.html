Isombe is a beloved Rwandan dish made from cassava leaves blended with groundnuts and sometimes mixed with dried fish or meat. It is a nutritious and flavorful staple in Rwandan households and often served with rice or Ubugari.

  4. Add cassava leaves, groundnut paste, and seasonings (e.g., salt, bouillon).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped cassava leaves
  • 1 cup ground peanuts (or peanut butter)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: dried fish or beef
