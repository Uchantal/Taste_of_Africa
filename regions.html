West Africa

West Africa is known for its vibrant flavors, rich stews, and use of local ingredients like yams, cassava, and rice. Dishes such as Jollof Rice and egusi are staple meals in countries like Nigeria, Ghana, and Senegal.

Signature Dishes

  • Jollof Rice
  • Egusi Soup

East Africa

East African cuisine incorporates the use of spices and grains like maize, millet, and sorghum. Signature dishes include Injera typically from Ethiopia and Ugali from Kenya. There are also isombe and ubugari as well as cassava and beans from Rwanda

Signature Dishes

  • Injera
  • Ugali
  • Isombe and Ubugari
  • Cassava and beans

North Africa

North African cuisine blends influences from Arabic, Berber, and Mediterranean cultures. Popular dishes include Tagine, a slow-cooked stew, and Couscous, served with lamb, vegetables, or chicken.

Signature Dishes

  • Tagine
  • Cassava and beans
